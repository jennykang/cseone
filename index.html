CSE One Connect and Share with EveryOne

About

From typical COMP course projects to the final-year projects, a very interesting pattern usually occurs: Groups are usually formed among local students, or formed by international students with similar nationality or cultural background.

To address this issue, a discussion group composing of local and international students was formed in Spring 2014. After a few iterations of discussion, we found out that cultural difference is only one of the symptoms. Students having similar ethic background usually share a similar mindset, and thus they are more eager to work with each other. This group setting is usually very efficient to solve well-defined problems, such as course projects.

However, solving real-life problems usually need creativity. CSE One strongly believes that creativity is originated by thoughtful discussion among members with diversified backgrounds. We believe that our ongoing activities, such as CSE TechShare, can help connect students from different backgrounds. We would also like to take this opportunity to build a family-like environment within the CSE community.


Contact us

E-mail: one@cse.ust.hk

Objectives

  1. To connect students from different backgrounds who are interested in computer science and information technology. Our target audience includes, but not limited to, COMP/CPEG students, students taking Dual Degree Program and year-1 engineering students who are interested

  2. To assist students in both academic and professional development, and help students explore multiple pathways upon graduation

  3. To promote caring spirit and help build long-term friendship among students
