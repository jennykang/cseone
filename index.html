Objectives

  • To assist new CSE students integrating to the CSE community.
  • To broaden the horizons of participants, especially CSE students, that there are multiple pathways upon graduation.
  • To increase studentsâ€™ technical competencies by exposing them to recent technology and sharing from students with industrial experience
  • To promote caring spirit and help build long term friendship among students in the mentorship.
  • To promote the ideology of CSE ONE, with the goal to connect everyone and promote multi-cultural awareness within the CSE community and pave way for future activities.
